Project Roadmap

Our comprehensive plan for the development and growth of the Stoned Rabbits NFT ecosystem.

Project Progress

40% Complete

Journey to the Moon

The Stoned Rabbits roadmap is our blueprint for creating an immersive NFT ecosystem. We're committed to delivering long-term value to our community through innovative features, exclusive benefits, and continuous development.

Phase 1: Genesis

Q2 2025

  • Project Inception

    April 2025

    Formation of the core team, concept development, and initial art creation for the Stoned Rabbits NFT collection.

  • Community Building

    June 2025

    Launch of social media channels, Discord server, and early community engagement initiatives. Building the initial community of supporters and enthusiasts.

  • Website Launch

    June 2025

    Development and deployment of the official Stoned Rabbits website, showcasing the project's vision, roadmap, and team.

  • NFT Collection Reveal

    June 2025

    Revealing the first preview of Stoned Rabbits NFT collection, showcasing the unique art style and traits. Announcing mint date and details.

Phase 2: Expansion

Q3 2025

  • Public Mint

    June 2025

    Launch of the public mint for Stoned Rabbits NFT collection. 3,333 unique NFTs will be available for minting on the Solana blockchain.

  • Marketplace Integration

    June 2025

    Integration with major NFT marketplaces including MageicEden. Implementation of royalty mechanisms to support ongoing project development.

  • DAO Formation

    September 2025

    Establishment of the Stoned Rabbits DAO, giving NFT holders voting rights on future project directions and treasury allocations. Implementation of governance mechanisms.

  • Exclusive Merchandise

    December 2025

    Launch of the official Stoned Rabbits merchandise store, featuring exclusive apparel and accessories only available to NFT holders.

Phase 3: Utility

Q4 2025

  • Community Treasury

    July 2025

    Establishment of the community treasury. The treasury will be used to fund future developments, staff salaries and community initiatives.

  • Launch Stoned Rabbits Plinko on Gamblor Casino

    August 2025

    The first Stoned Rabbits Plinko game will be launched on Gamblor Casino and a part of the profits will be share with the holders

  • $GMBL Token Airdrop

    October 2025

    $GMBL token will be the casino native token. Stoned Rabbit holders will get an airdrop of this token.

  • $STONED Token Launch

    October 2025

    Introduction of the $STONED utility token, airdropped to NFT holders. The token will be used for governance, staking, and accessing exclusive features within the ecosystem.

  • Staking Mechanism

    November 2025

    Implementation of NFT staking, allowing holders to earn $STONED tokens by staking their Stoned Rabbits NFTs. Different rarity levels will have different earning rates.

  • Strategic Partnerships

    November 2025

    Announcing strategic partnerships with other NFT projects, DeFi protocols, and Web3 platforms to expand the Stoned Rabbits ecosystem and provide additional utility to holders.

Phase 4: TBD

Q1 2026

  • Future Roadmap Expansion

    Q1 2026

    Community voting on the next phase of the roadmap, with new features and initiatives proposed and selected by the DAO.
