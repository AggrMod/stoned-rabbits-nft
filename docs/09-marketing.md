## Brand Identity

### Brand Positioning

**Tagline:** *"Stoned Rabbits: Where Community Meets Revenue"*

**Value Proposition:**
- **For Holders:** Passive income through revenue sharing + vibrant community
- **For Projects:** Professional NFT utilities at affordable prices
- **For Gamblers:** Fun slot machines with unique Stoned Rabbits theme

### Brand Voice

- **Tone:** Fun, relaxed, community-focused, transparent
- **Style:** Casual but professional, meme-friendly
- **Values:** Community first, sustainable revenue, builder mindset

### Visual Identity

**Colors:**
- Primary Green: `#237253` (trust, growth)
- Orange: `#ed683e` (energy, excitement)
- Gold: `#ffd166` (premium, rewards)

**Imagery:**
- Stoned rabbit characters
- Cannabis/chill theme
- Slot machine aesthetics
- Space/cosmic backgrounds

## Target Audience

### Primary Audiences

**1. NFT Collectors**
- Age: 18-35
- Interest: NFT art, gaming, crypto
- Behavior: Active on Discord/Twitter
- Goal: Find undervalued projects with utility

**2. Passive Income Seekers**
- Age: 25-45
- Interest: Crypto income streams, staking
- Behavior: Research-focused, long-term holders
- Goal: Diversify income sources

**3. NFT Project Founders**
- Age: 20-40
- Interest: Building NFT projects
- Behavior: Budget-conscious, need tech solutions
- Goal: Launch affordable utilities for their community

**4. Gamblers/Casino Players**
- Age: 21-50
- Interest: Online gambling, crypto casinos
- Behavior: Play regularly, follow favorite themes
- Goal: Entertainment + potential wins

## Partnership Strategy

### Target Partners

**1. NFT Projects**
- Similar size (1k-5k holders)
- Aligned values
- Complementary utilities
- Collaboration: Cross-promotion, joint giveaways

**2. Solana Ecosystem**
- DEXs (Jupiter, Raydium)
- Wallets (Phantom, Solflare)
- Launchpads (Magic Eden, Tensor)
- Collaboration: Featured listings, integrations

**3. Casinos/Gaming**
- Gamblor.io (current partner)
- Other crypto casinos
- Game studios
- Collaboration: Exclusive slots, rev share

**4. Utility Providers**
- Staking platforms
- DAO tools

- Collaboration: Service exchange, referrals

## Metrics & KPIs

### Key Performance Indicators

**Growth Metrics:**
- Twitter followers (Goal: +100/week)
- Discord members (Goal: +50/week)
- Website visitors (Goal: 1000/week)
- Email subscribers (Goal: +25/week)

**Engagement Metrics:**
- Twitter engagement rate (Goal: >3%)
- Discord daily active users (Goal: >100)
- Content shares (Goal: >50/week)

**Revenue Metrics:**
- NFT floor price (Goal: Stable/increasing)
- Trading volume (Goal: >100 SOL/week)
- Utility Factory clients (Goal: 2/month)

**Community Metrics:**
- Holder retention (Goal: >90%)
- Community sentiment (Monitor mentions)
- Support ticket response time (Goal: <24h)
