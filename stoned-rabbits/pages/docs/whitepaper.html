Introduction

Stoned Rabbits is a collection of 10,000 unique Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs) living on the Solana blockchain. Each Stoned Rabbit is programmatically generated from over 200 possible traits, including background, fur, eyes, mouth, clothing, accessories, and more. The combination of these traits creates unique, one-of-a-kind digital collectibles with varying levels of rarity.

This whitepaper outlines the vision, features, tokenomics, and roadmap for the Stoned Rabbits ecosystem. It serves as a comprehensive guide for community members, investors, and interested parties to understand the project's goals, mechanics, and future plans.

Stoned Rabbits aims to bridge the gap between digital art, blockchain technology, and community building. Through innovative features, engaging experiences, and community-driven development, we're creating a project that provides long-term value to holders and contributes to the evolution of the NFT space.

Project Background

The concept for Stoned Rabbits was born in early 2025, inspired by the vibrant culture of digital art and the possibilities offered by blockchain technology. Our team of artists, developers, and blockchain enthusiasts came together with a shared vision: to create not just another NFT collection, but a comprehensive ecosystem that provides genuine utility and value to its community.

Drawing inspiration from both traditional art and digital culture, the Stoned Rabbits collection features unique characters with distinct personalities and traits. Each rabbit represents a member of a whimsical world where creativity, community, and innovation are paramount.

NFT Collection

Collection Overview

The Stoned Rabbits collection consists of 10,000 unique NFTs, each representing a distinct rabbit character with various traits and attributes. These NFTs are stored as SPL tokens on the Solana blockchain, ensuring ownership, transferability, and interoperability within the broader NFT ecosystem.

Each Stoned Rabbit is programmatically generated from a combination of over 200 possible traits across multiple categories:

  • Background (20 variations)
  • Fur Color & Type (25 variations)
  • Eyes (15 variations)
  • Mouth (15 variations)
  • Clothing (30 variations)
  • Headwear (25 variations)
  • Accessories (30 variations)
  • Special Features (40 variations)

The combination of these traits results in a wide variety of unique characters, each with its own appearance, style, and personality. Some traits are rarer than others, creating a natural rarity hierarchy within the collection.

Rarity System

Stoned Rabbits features a tiered rarity system that determines the scarcity of each NFT based on its trait combination:

  • Common (50%): NFTs with standard trait combinations.
  • Uncommon (30%): NFTs with at least one rare trait.
  • Rare (15%): NFTs with multiple rare traits or at least one very rare trait.
  • Epic (4%): NFTs with unique combinations of very rare traits.
  • Legendary (1%): NFTs with the rarest trait combinations, often including exclusive traits not found in other categories.

Each NFT's rarity score is calculated based on the rarity of its individual traits, creating a transparent and fair system for determining value within the collection.

Metadata & Technical Specifications

Each Stoned Rabbit NFT includes comprehensive metadata stored on IPFS (InterPlanetary File System), ensuring that the data remains accessible and immutable. The metadata includes:

  • Unique ID
  • Name
  • Description
  • Image URL (stored on IPFS)
  • Detailed trait information
  • Rarity score and tier
  • Generation timestamp

The NFTs are minted using a secure, optimized Metaplex-compatible program on the Solana blockchain. The program includes the following features:

  • Provenance hash to verify the randomness and fairness of the generation process
  • Royalty support for secondary sales
  • Secure minting mechanism with anti-bot protection
  • Metadata freezing after reveal to ensure immutability
  • Ownership verification for exclusive features and utilities

The Stoned Rabbits ecosystem includes a utility token, $SRBBT, which serves as the primary currency within our platform. $SRBBT is an SPL token on the Solana blockchain with a fixed total supply of 100,000,000 tokens.

Token Utility

The $SRBBT token serves multiple functions within the Stoned Rabbits ecosystem:

  • Governance: $SRBBT holders can vote on proposals and decisions that affect the project's development and direction.
  • Staking: NFT holders can stake their Stoned Rabbits to earn $SRBBT rewards.
  • Access: Exclusive features, events, and areas within the metaverse require $SRBBT to access.
  • Marketplace: $SRBBT is used for transactions within the Stoned Rabbits marketplace, including purchasing NFTs, merchandise, and digital assets.
  • Breeding: In future phases, $SRBBT will be used for breeding new Stoned Rabbits with unique traits.
  • In-game Currency: $SRBBT will serve as the primary currency in Stoned Rabbits games and experiences.

Tokenomics Sustainability

To ensure the long-term sustainability of the $SRBBT token economy, we've implemented several mechanisms:

  • Deflationary Mechanisms: A portion of tokens used for transactions and breeding are burned, reducing the circulating supply over time.
  • Staking Incentives: Long-term staking is rewarded with higher APY, encouraging holders to lock their tokens and reduce selling pressure.
  • Utility Expansion: We continuously develop new utilities for $SRBBT to maintain and increase demand.
  • Treasury Management: The project treasury actively manages token reserves to ensure price stability and sustainable growth.

DAO Governance

Governance Structure

The Stoned Rabbits DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization) gives the community direct input into the project's development and future. The governance structure is designed to be fair, transparent, and efficient, balancing the need for community input with the ability to make timely decisions.

The DAO governance structure includes:

  • Proposal System: Any DAO member can submit proposals for consideration by the community.
  • Voting Mechanism: Proposals are voted on by token holders, with voting power determined by both NFT ownership and $SRBBT token holdings.
  • Execution Framework: Approved proposals are implemented by the core team or designated community members.
  • Treasury Management: The community treasury is managed through DAO governance, with transparency and accountability.

Voting Power

Voting power in the DAO is determined by a combination of factors:

  • NFT Ownership: Each Stoned Rabbit NFT provides base voting power, with rarer NFTs granting more voting power.
  • $SRBBT Holdings: Holding and staking $SRBBT tokens provides additional voting power.
  • Participation Bonuses: Active community members can earn participation bonuses that increase their voting power.

This hybrid model ensures that both early supporters (NFT holders) and active participants (token holders and community contributors) have a meaningful say in governance decisions.

Proposal Process

The proposal process follows these steps:

  1. Ideation: Community members discuss potential proposals in designated forums and channels.
  2. Drafting: A formal proposal is drafted, including detailed information on the proposed changes, implementation plan, budget requirements, and expected outcomes.
  3. Submission: The proposal is submitted to the DAO platform for consideration.
  4. Discussion Period: A mandatory discussion period allows community members to ask questions, suggest improvements, and debate the proposal.
  5. Voting Period: After the discussion period, the proposal enters a voting period where eligible members can cast their votes.
  6. Execution: If approved, the proposal is implemented according to the specified timeline and plan.
  7. Monitoring: The implementation is monitored, and results are reported back to the community.

Treasury Management

The DAO treasury serves as the financial backbone of the project, funded through:

  • Initial allocation of $SRBBT tokens
  • Secondary sales royalties from NFTs
  • Revenue from ecosystem activities
  • Partnerships and collaborations

Treasury funds are used for:

  • Project development and maintenance
  • Marketing and community growth
  • Partnerships and collaborations
  • Community rewards and incentives
  • Special projects and initiatives

All treasury activities are transparent and subject to DAO governance, with regular financial reports provided to the community.
