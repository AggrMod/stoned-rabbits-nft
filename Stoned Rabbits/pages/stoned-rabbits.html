About the Collection

Welcome to the gang

Stoned Rabbits is a collection of 3,333 unique NFTs living on the Solana blockchain. Each Stoned Rabbit is entirely unique and algorithmically generated from over 200 possible traits.

Our mission is to create an exclusive community of like-minded individuals who appreciate digital art and the possibilities of blockchain technology. By owning a Stoned Rabbit, you're not just collecting digital art - you're joining a movement.

Holder Benefits

  • Access to exclusive poker tournaments
  • Treasure hunts and prize raffles
  • Future airdrops and rewards
  • Voting rights in community decisions
